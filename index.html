About

My name is Yuanbo Li, I'm a CS Ph.D student at Brown University. Before that, I graduated from Columbia University with B.A in Mathematics. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Daniel Ritchie at Brown Visual Computing Lab.

I'm generally interested in creating tools to help designers quickly explore varies ideas at early stages. Currently, I'm mostly working on using VLMs with other foundation models to achieve this goal in a neural-symbolic way.
